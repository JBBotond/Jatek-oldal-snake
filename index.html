    How to play?

  • Control the snake toward the apples using the arrows.
  • Lock the screen by clicking on the play window
  • With each eaten apple the snake gets larger and larger.
  • If the snake hits the wall or it's own body, it's Game Over.
  • Try your best and get the highest score possible!
